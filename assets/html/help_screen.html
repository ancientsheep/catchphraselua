Classic Mode (Hot Potato)

Overview:
Players take turns passing the device & giving clues in order to help teammates guess mystery words & phrases before the timer runs out. Whichever team isn't stuck holding the device when the buzzer goes off scores a point. Whoever has the most points at the end of the game, wins.

  • Pass after a single, correct answer
  • Only Team members guess
  • Points awarded for not getting stuck with the device
  • High Score wins
Set-up:
  • Divide players into two even teams.
  • Sit in a circle so everyone is sitting next to a member of the opposite team. (ex: A-B-A-B-A-B-A-B)

  • You may give a team a "handicap" by altering the score here if you wish.
Gameplay:
  • Randomly choose a player to go first.
  • A game typically consists of multiple rounds.
  • Press "Start Round" to begin.
  • A word or phrase will appear on screen & a timer will begin counting down.
  • Get your team to guess the words by giving verbal & gestural clues. Only your team should guess.
  • If you're stuck on a word, you may choose to "skip," assuming your group allowed for skips during game set-up.
  • As soon as your teammates guess the correct answer, carefully hand the device to the next player of the opposing team (Never throw or toss the device).
  • Play continues with the new player pressing the "next" button.
  • Players continue this process until the buzzer sounds & flashes on screen. The timer is slightly randomized, so there's no telling exactly when it will go off.
  • Whichever team isn't in possession of the device when the buzzer sound gets 1 point. (Note a device is considered "handed off" as soon as it touches the hands of the next player).
  • That team also gets a single chance to guess the mystery phrase if it hasn't yet been said aloud (they may discuss it briefly first). If they guess correctly, the team also receives 1 bonus point, for a total of 2 points. The losing team awards these points.
  • The next round begins with whoever was holding the device when the previous round ended.
  • Play continues until the victory condition of the game is met. At that point, the team with the highest score wins.
The Clue Giver Cannot:
  • Say a word that rhymes with the word
  • Spell out any part of the word
  • Give "Sounds like" clues
  • Say any word or phrase on the screen unless a teammate says it first.
  • Skip a word using the "next" button.
  • Throw, Toss, or Slide the device to another player.
If the other team catches you doing any of the things listed above, immediately pause the game, "end round," and manually award the other team a single point. Then proceed to the next round. If a scoring error is made, players can manually adjust the score at any point between rounds.
